Test Preparation Guide

Comprehensive resources and tips for IELTS, TOEFL, GRE, and PTE to help you achieve your target score

IELTS

International English Language Testing System

2 hours 45 minutes 2 years validity

Test Structure

Sections: Listening, Reading, Writing, Speaking
Score Range: Band 0-9

Recommended Resources

  • Official IELTS practice materials
  • British Council IELTS prep
  • Cambridge IELTS series (Books 1-18)
  • IELTS Liz (free online resources)

Preparation Tips

  1. 1. Practice with authentic IELTS materials

  2. 2. Focus on time management in each section

  3. 3. Record yourself speaking to identify weaknesses

  4. 4. Read academic articles and newspapers daily

  5. 5. Practice writing Task 1 and Task 2 essays regularly

TOEFL

Test of English as a Foreign Language

3 hours 2 years validity

Test Structure

Sections: Reading, Listening, Speaking, Writing
Score Range: 0-120 points

Recommended Resources

  • ETS Official TOEFL Guide
  • TOEFL iBT Practice Sets
  • Magoosh TOEFL Prep

Preparation Tips

  • 1
    Familiarize with computer format
  • 2
    Build stamina for 3-hour test
  • 3
    Practice listening without seeing questions
  • 4
    Take full-length practice tests under timed conditions
  • 5
    Master note-taking techniques

GRE

Graduate Record Examination

3h 45m 5 years validity

Test Structure

Sections: Verbal, Quant, Analytical Writing
Score Range: Verbal/Quant: 130-170, Writing: 0-6

Recommended Resources

  • ETS Official GRE Guide
  • Manhattan Prep GRE series
  • Gregmat (online prep course)

Preparation Tips

  • 1
    Build a strong vocabulary (use flashcards)
  • 2
    Practice quantitative reasoning problems daily
  • 3
    Analyze sample essays for scoring criteria
  • 4
    Take adaptive practice tests
  • 5
    Focus on both speed and accuracy

General Test-Taking Strategies

Before the Test

  • Start preparation 3-6 months in advance
  • Take diagnostic tests to identify weaknesses
  • Create a structured study schedule
  • Join study groups or online communities

During the Test

  • Read instructions carefully
  • Manage time effectively for each section
  • Don't spend too long on difficult questions
  • Stay calm and focused throughout

After the Test

  • Review your performance honestly
  • Identify areas for improvement
  • Consider retaking if needed
  • Send scores to universities promptly
